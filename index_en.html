About Us

Silent Witness is driven by a multidisciplinary team passionate about ethics, technology, and human rights. Our mission is to prevent extreme distress situations, especially youth suicide, by detecting often invisible signals.

The Idea

Silent Witness analyzes weak signals such as voice frequency, unusual movements, and digital behaviors. These data are classified by alert level. When a critical threshold is reached, a secure alert is sent to the competent authorities, NGOs, and hospitals.

All this is done within a strict ethical framework respecting privacy, with progressive monitoring from detection to appropriate intervention.

Objectives

  • Prevent suicides and save lives through early detection
  • Reduce emergency intervention times
  • Ensure full confidentiality and ethics compliance
  • Collaborate effectively with NGOs, police, and hospitals

FAQ

Does the AI access personal data?
No. The analysis only covers anonymized and non-identifiable signals.
How are alerts sent?
Alerts are securely transmitted only to authorized partners in case of emergency.
