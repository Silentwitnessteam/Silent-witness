About Us

Silent Witness is led by a multidisciplinary team passionate about ethics, technology, and human rights. Our mission is to prevent invisible distress situations, especially suicide risks among young people.

Concept

Silent Witness analyzes weak signals: vocal frequency, unusual movements, and digital searches. This information is classified according to its level of danger. When a critical threshold is reached, an ethical alert is sent to authorities, NGOs, or partner hospitals.

Everything is done with strict respect for privacy: the AI acts to save, not to spy.

The process is progressive: detection → monitoring → adapted intervention.

Objectives

  • Save lives by early detection of distress situations
  • Reduce intervention delays
  • Strictly respect confidentiality and ethics
  • Collaborate with NGOs, police, and hospitals for effective care
  • Build a secure database of digital evidence to support investigations

FAQ

Does the AI access personal data?
No. Silent Witness analyzes only anonymized signals and fully respects confidentiality.
How are alerts sent?
Alerts are securely transmitted to partner authorities and NGOs only in critical situations.
Is this an active service or a prototype?
Currently, Silent Witness is in an advanced development phase, with pilot tests planned soon.
Who can contact you?
Any organization, professional, or individual wishing to collaborate or get information can write to us.
